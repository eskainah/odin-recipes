Liberian Traditional Recipes

Before we dive into our recipes for today, I figure out that it will be best if I give you a brief history about the Liberian people.
The people of Liberia are classified into three major groups: the indigenous people, who are in the majority and who migrated
from the western Sudan in the late Middle Ages; Black immigrants from the United States (known historically as Americo-Liberians)
and the West Indies; and other Black immigrants from neighbouring western African states who came during the anti-slave-trade
campaign and European colonial rule.
