Dry Rice

Liberian Dry Rice is a seasoned finger-licking white rice mixed with kittely (African Eggplant),
okra, dry rice spice and a variety of meat, fish or chicken that is served along with the dish using
during the weekend.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Vita Cubes
  • Oil Can Sardines
  • Luncheon meat
  • Fresh Fish
  • Kittely
  • Okra
  • Pepper

Steps

The steps to fellow when preparing this soup is quite simple

  1. Season fish to your liking, then fry fish first and set aside.
  2. Boil Rice, you can add kittely and okra in the rice or separately.
  3. When the rice is cooked, mash kittely, okra, pepper and vita cubes together in a bowl
  4. Mix rice in with the kittely/okra paste
  5. Add sardine and luncheon meat on top or mix it with your rice.
  6. Liberian Dry Rice with Fry Fish is ready to be served! If you have red oil, put some on top with your fish for a delicious meal!
