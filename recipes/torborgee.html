Torborgee

There are approximately 10 types of Torborgee soups that is cook in Liberia. Here is the recipes for Beans Torborgee.
This types of torborgee that is popular among all the ethnic groups of Liberia.

Beans torborgee is a spicy Liberian stew made with beans and a special oil called the torborgee palm oil.
It is orginally associated with the Lorma people inhabiting the area of Lofa County in Liberia.
